The filter row & the plain-English summary

The PRD's logic is OR within a group, AND across groups. Rather than make you decode that, the bar narrates itself: a live sentence tells you exactly what the shelf is showing. Cyan words are OR-choices, pink words are AND-narrowing. State pills that are hidden-by-default (Completed · Platinum · Dropped) use a dashed outline — they reveal a hidden slice; the solid cyan flag pills narrow what's shown.

Because you're “not sure yet”: State + Genre default to friendly labels (“All live statuses”, “Any genre”), the reveal pills sit quietly dashed until needed, and the sentence teaches you the model by just… telling you. If you later find you only ever tap two things, we promote those to always-visible and bury the rest — easy change.
