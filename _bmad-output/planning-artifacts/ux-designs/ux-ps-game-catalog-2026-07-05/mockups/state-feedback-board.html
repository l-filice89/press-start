How PRESS START talks back — the four channels

Toast for good news that passes, summary modal for reports on things you triggered, attention banner for things that need you (persists), loading for work in flight. One rule: a triggered op resolves into a summary modal, and anything needing action also seeds the banner so it survives the dismiss.

Fire on quick shelf actions. Never demand a click. Bottom of screen, stack, fade.

The rule in action
why a modal and a banner
You read the summary and hit Done. The good news is gone — but that ⚠ 1 needs attention doesn't vanish: it drops into the attention banner under the header, waiting, until you resolve it. Nothing that needs you is ever one dismissed-modal away from being forgotten.

3 · Attention banner — persistent, under the header
The home for anything that needs action. Stays until cleared. This is also where the PRD's "failures surface, never silently retry" lives.
